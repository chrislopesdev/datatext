About DataText Event Services

DataText was formed to compete in the personnel segment of the trade/consumer show and conference industry. Since our incorporation in 2002 DataText has offered quality staffing at competitive rates.

By delivering intelligent, enthusiastic personnel, in addition to our own hands-on motivated customer service experience we add value to our clients’ events. The owners Tom & Donna Newberry and our management team know customer service; we possess a wealth of experience that will have a positive impact on the events your organization services. Our ideals of effective service have been reinforced by the feedback received from both our local and international clients, who acknowledge that we have delivered on their demands.

We add value to our clients’ events.

We consider our service to be the industry standard and as an industry leader we believe our dedication to high performance will benefit your organization's events.
